Gastos.ph - Money Tracking App

Gastos.ph is an app for tracking a user's cash flow. A user can input and categorize their sources of income and expenses. They can easily compare and analyze with charts generated by the app from the data they provided. This budgeting app can help an everyday Filipino in tracking their monthly, quarterly and yearly gastusin.

Panahoony - Weather App

Panahoony is a weather app displaying weather stats of the three main cities of the Philippines. The name of the app is derived from the word Panahon—which means weather in Filipino—and Hoony, the name of the animated character on the app. Aside from the stats being displayed, the weather can be visually determined by how the animated character is presented.

Doowit - Task Management App

Doowit is a task management app where a user can easily catalog their todo's—collate those in progress, those that are done, and those that are overdue. Tasks are labeled based on priority. An option to add the task's due date is also available.

Old School NBA - Streaming App

The main concept of this streaming app is it serves as a repository of the earliest recorded NBA games in the history of the game up to the early 2000s. While not all games were recorded, this app is a good place to relive those glory days of classic NBA.
