Mars Rovers - Homepage Design

A homepage, more often than not, is the first thing users see when they visit a website. Not everyone gets excited when it comes to robots and spaceships, some may even scare away just by mentioning Science. But a website for Science doesn't have to be presented like a college textbook.

Explore Web prototype on Figma »

Prototype Instructions: click to scroll down, drag to scroll up.

Homepage Experience
To entice Science fans and non-Science fans alike, the homepage for the Mars Rovers website is laid out in a way that it goes through each section of the homepage similar as to how the rovers journey through the Red Planet. Text-based information would also need to be trimmed down to bite-sized content to not overload the user with information.
