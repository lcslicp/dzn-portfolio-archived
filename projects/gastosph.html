Gastos.ph - Mobile UI Design

Gastos.ph is a Money Tracking App designed to help the user in tracking their cash flow. While personal finance resources are now easily accessible, not everyone knows where to start. This app is a good place to start in tracking one's budget and building a good relationship with money.

Welcome Kit
After creating an account, new users of the app will be greeted with a Welcome Kit with an option to skip. Creating an account is necessary to store and protect their data.

Homepage at a Glance
The app's homepage is the gateway to all of the app's features. At a glance, the user can view the last updated inputs, comparison charts, transaction history and navigation buttons to explore the app.

Data Input
The plus (+) button is pressed to add new data input whether it's to add Income or Expense. The user has the option to choose a Category and add a Label on top of the inputted amount. There is also the option to automate recurring Income/Expense data inputs such as mortgage and fixed salaries.
