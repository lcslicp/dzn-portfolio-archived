Old School NBA - Streaming App

Wouldn't it be cool to have a streaming app where you can find NBA games dating back to the 90s, 80s or even the 60s? As a fan of old school NBA, it would be great to find them all in one place. This is what the app is all about—a streaming app for old school NBA games.

Decreased Cognitive Load
A user is greeted with an array of curated videos upon launching the homepage. This is to reduce the cognitive load of the user. They don't have to think too much about choosing which video or game to watch, the app has already laid it out for them.

Thumbnail
One way of enticing a user to watch a video on a streaming app is through its thumbnail. Thumbnails are selected in a way that they capture the action of the game enough to attract viewers to click on the video.
