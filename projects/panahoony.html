Panahoony - Weather App

A weather app displays detailed weather conditions and weather forecasts of a certain place. For this particular app, it displays the weather conditions of the three main cities of the Philippines—Manila, Cebu and Davao. Hoony, the animated character added in the background, renders according to the real-time reading of the weather conditions displayed.

Character Hoony
Hoony is a nonchalant-looking young Filipino. Illustrated on Adobe Illustrator and animated on After Effects, this character will be integrated into the app using Lottie.The animated character is a separate entity from the accompanying animated background which also shows the weather conditions. These animations are particularly short in duration to minimize its file size. It is important to consider that the animations need to be lightweight for the app to load at its intended pace.

React Project
Panahoony is a recreation of a previous weather app project I've worked on that has already been deployed. It's a simple weather app for Cebu that also has the same structure of conditionally rendering animations in the background based on the weather conditions displayed. This was built with React JS, fetching stats from WeatherAPI.
